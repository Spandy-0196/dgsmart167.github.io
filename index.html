Introducing the Team Leader......

The Leader of The Team 167 of LIC of India, Mr. Dilip Ghoshal, joined the organization in the year 1992. He has been working solely on Human Resource Management, his personal area or sphere of expertise since his induction to this eminent organization of Lic of India in 1992. At Present, he leads a Highly Professional Team of near about 100 Insurance Advisors and Financial Planners, thereby insuring the lives of more than 25000 Valued Customers along with an attached AUM (Assets Under Management) portfolio of more than 1000 crores. He has converted numerous ordinary personnel belonging to any quota of the Present Human Society, into Successful and Established Insurance Advisors and Financial Planners, thereby enabling them reach the zenith of their career in life. Mr. Dilip Ghoshal is also the Director of the MDRT Academy of Howrah, which in itself is a House of Creating Successful MDRTs (Million Dollar Round Table) personnel. He also is a mentor of the Pioneer Club of Howrah and an active member of the All India Ace Development Officers Group. He has had the honour of receiving awards from the Chairman and the Zonal Manager of the Esteemed Organization he belongs to. A bold, dynamic and successful team leader, being committed to work always, Mr. Ghoshal possesses a State of The Art Fully Air Conditioned Personal Office Cum Training Centre 'UJJIBAN' also known as 'I-SMART' at Andul Road, Howrah.
